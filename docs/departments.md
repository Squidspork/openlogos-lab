# Departments

OpenLogos Lab is organized as a small research institution.

## Board Room

- `Dr. Logos`: Research Director / orchestrator.
- `Scout`: Opportunity Discovery.
- `Nemo`: Scientific Inquiry.
- `Prospect`: Product Applications.
- `Control`: Risk and Ethics.

## Research Pipeline

- Director Office.
- Opportunity Discovery.
- Scientific Inquiry.
- Product Applications.
- Human Testing and Simulation.
- Risk and Ethics.
- Publications.

Each department owns its prompt notes, schema alias, tools placeholder, README, and agent code.
